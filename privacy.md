 ## 1. Information We Collect

Otisdub Tag PLUS is an online multiplayer VR game. To function correctly, the App collects the following types of data:

### a) Player Display Name

- Players choose a **display name** that is visible to other players in multiplayer sessions.

- Players are responsible for choosing their own display name.

- A display name **may include real-world information if the player chooses to include it,** but this is not required and can be changed at any time in-game.

### b) Player ID (PlayFab)

- The App generates a **random, persistent Player ID** using PlayFab.

- This Player ID:

  - Is not a real name or email address

  - Is used to uniquely identify a player

  - Does not change over time

- The Player ID is used for multiplayer, progression, and backend services.

### c) Multiplayer & Gameplay Data

- Multiplayer session participation

- Lobby and matchmaking data

- Basic gameplay information required for online play

This data is necessary for online multiplayer functionality.

### d) Voice Chat Data

- Otisdub Tag PLUS uses voice chat for in-game communication.

- Voice audio is transmitted in real time only.

- **Voice chat audio is NOT recorded, stored, or saved** by the App or by Otisclub.

Collected data is used **only** to:

- Enable online multiplayer

- Identify players in-game

- Display player-chosen display names

- Support voice chat communication

- Maintain stable and secure gameplay services

We do **not:**

- Track users across apps or websites

- Collect emails, phone numbers, or real names directly

## 4. Data Sharing

Player data is shared **only** with:

- Required third-party service providers (PlayFab, Photon)

- Platform services required for Meta Quest functionality

No data is shared with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

## 5. Data Protection & Security

Otisclub takes data protection seriously and applies the following safeguards:

- Use of reputable third-party backend providers with industry-standard security

- No storage of voice recordings

- No collection of unnecessary personal information

- Access to player data limited to what is required to operate the App

## 6. Children and Teen Users (13+)

Otisdub Tag PLUS is intended for a Teen (13+) audience.

- The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

- Players should not include real-world personal information in their display names.

## 7. Data Retention

- Player IDs and multiplayer data are retained as long as the account exists.

- Voice chat audio is **not retained.**

- Display names can be changed by the player at any time.
